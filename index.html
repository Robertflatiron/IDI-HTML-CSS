About

Ideal Diagnostic Imaging is a freestanding medical facility providing diagnostic imaging services to patients in the Dallas area. Why are we different than a hospital which provides the same diagnostic services? The main difference between our facility and a hospital is cost. Hospitals are allowed to charge Medicare and other commercial insurers a facility fee, which can result in a cost 3-4 times the amount as our cost. All this while providing the same imaging service. We are here because we care about you and are here to provide a way for you to save money all while receiving the same much needed medical treatment.
